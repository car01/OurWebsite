Bristol

Ronni has spent most of her life living in the beautiful city of Bristol.

Located in the South-West of England, it is a 2 hour drive from London and has its own airport. It is known for its unique culture, arts and architecture.

Bars

Bristol is full of bars, from cocktail bars to relaxed pubs. If you want something a bit different, check out its numerous speakeasy bars. If you want something more traditional, Charlie's is a classic British pub offering a varied selection of drinks and food

Restaurants

Restaurants in Bristol range from very modern to very traditional restaurants. Brown's Restaurant is next to Bristol's Museum and Art Gallery. It used to be the former Museum and Library.

Cafes

Cafes in Bristol range from traditional British cafes to modern and international cafes. The Tea Room is a great place to enjoy a cup of tea and delicious scones.

Why we love Bristol

Bristol is a great place to experience traditional British culture. You can also take part in its many festivals throughout the year, such as its world-famous Bristol Balloon Fiesta and the annual Harbour Festival

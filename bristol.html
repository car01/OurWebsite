Bristol

Ronni has spent most of her life living in the beautiful city of Bristol.

Located in the South-West of England, it is a 2 hour drive from London and has its own airport. It is known for its unique culture, arts and architecture.

Bars

Bristol is full of bars, from cocktail bars to relaxed pubs. If you want something a bit different, check out its numerous speakeasy bars

Charlie's is a traditional British pub offering a varied selection of drinks and food

Restaurants

Restaurants in Bristol range from very modern to very traditional restaurants

Brown's Restaurant is next to Bristol's Museum and Art Gallery. It used to be the former Museum and Library.

Cafes

There is an unlimited choice of cute little cafes in Paris.

A well know cafe which represents the culture of Paris is Cafe de Paris, located in Geneve, a region of Paris.

Conclusion

Paris is a great place to stay, offering many interesting things to cater to everyone's needs!
